Privacy Policy

Last updated: 22 April 2026

Overview

FastPlan ("we", "our", "the app") is built by FastPlan Co. Your privacy matters. This policy explains what data we collect, how it's used, and the controls you have. FastPlan does not require an account, and most of your data stays on your device.

Data We Collect

  • Health & Fitness data — fasting times, weight, and nutrition entries you log. Stored locally on your device. If you grant access, FastPlan reads from and writes to Apple Health to sync this information.
  • Photos — when you use the AI food scanner, photos you take are sent securely to our processing service to recognise the food. Photos are not stored after analysis.
  • Purchase data — managed by RevenueCat to verify your subscription or one-time purchase. We never see or store your payment card details — Apple handles all payments.
  • Anonymous device identifiers — used by RevenueCat to associate purchases with your device so your subscription works across reinstalls.
  • Crash and performance data — collected anonymously by Expo to help us fix bugs and improve stability.

What We Don't Collect

  • We do not collect your name, email, phone number, or address.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not sell or share your personal data with advertisers.
  • We do not require you to create an account.

How Your Data Is Used

Your health, fitness, and nutrition data are used solely to power FastPlan's features — tracking your fasts, displaying your progress, and syncing with Apple Health. Photos sent to the AI food scanner are used only to identify the food and return nutrition information. Purchase data is used to keep your subscription active.

Apple Health

If you choose to connect Apple Health, FastPlan reads and writes data such as weight and dietary information. You can revoke this permission at any time from the iOS Settings app under Privacy & Security → Health → FastPlan.

Third-Party Services

  • RevenueCat — handles subscription and purchase validation. RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Apple App Store — processes all payments. Apple Privacy Policy
  • Expo — provides crash reporting and performance diagnostics. Expo Privacy Policy

Data Storage and Security

Your fasting, weight, and nutrition data are stored locally on your device. Data sent to third-party services (food photos, purchase verification) is transmitted over encrypted HTTPS connections.

Your Rights

You can delete all FastPlan data at any time by uninstalling the app. To revoke specific permissions (Apple Health, Camera, Photos), use the iOS Settings app. You can request deletion of any data held by our third-party processors by contacting us at the email below.

Children's Privacy

FastPlan is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top will reflect any changes. Significant changes will be communicated within the app.
